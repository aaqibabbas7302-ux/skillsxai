# QA Automation Tester Salary in India 2026: Complete Breakdown

QA Automation is one of the best-paying career paths in the Indian IT industry. With every company investing in test automation to ship faster, the demand for skilled automation testers has never been higher.

This guide breaks down exactly what you can expect to earn at every stage of your career.

## Salary by Experience Level

| Experience | Salary Range | Average |
|-----------|-------------|---------|
| Fresher (0-1 year) | ₹3.5L–₹6L/year | ₹4.5L |
| Junior (1-3 years) | ₹5L–₹10L/year | ₹7L |
| Mid-level (3-5 years) | ₹8L–₹16L/year | ₹12L |
| Senior (5-8 years) | ₹14L–₹25L/year | ₹18L |
| Lead/Architect (8+ years) | ₹20L–₹35L/year | ₹27L |

## Salary by Company Type

**Product companies** (Flipkart, Swiggy, Razorpay): Pay 30-50% more than service companies. Mid-level roles offer ₹12L–₹20L.

**IT service companies** (TCS, Infosys, Wipro): Standard pay bands. Mid-level roles offer ₹8L–₹14L. High job stability.

**Startups** (funded Series A+): Competitive salaries plus ESOPs. Mid-level: ₹10L–₹18L.

**MNCs** (Google, Microsoft, Amazon): Highest pay. Mid-level: ₹15L–₹30L. Rigorous hiring process.

**Remote/International**: $40K–$100K/year for companies hiring from India. The biggest salary multiplier.

## How to Maximize Your Salary

1. **Learn modern tools** — Playwright and Cypress are in higher demand than Selenium alone
2. **Build a portfolio** — GitHub repositories showing real test frameworks impress employers
3. **Get certified** — ISTQB Foundation + Agile Testing certifications add credibility
4. **Contribute to open source** — even small contributions to Playwright or Cypress repos stand out
5. **Negotiate with data** — use salary surveys and competing offers to negotiate better
6. **Consider remote roles** — international remote positions pay 3-5x Indian market rates
